01 — Profile

Who I Am

Engineering rigor meets domain depth — from orbit mechanics to LLM orchestration.

I am a senior aerospace engineer and systems developer with a strong focus on applied geospatial intelligence, autonomous AI architectures, and full-stack spatial data platforms. My work sits at the intersection of satellite remote sensing, machine learning, and modern cloud infrastructure.

As a specialist and consultant, I design and build end-to-end solutions — from satellite data ingestion pipelines using Sentinel-2 / SAR imagery, through spatial analysis stacks with PostGIS + GeoServer, to multi-agent AI systems orchestrated via NATS and running on local LLMs via Ollama.

My infrastructure work spans privileged Docker environments with eBPF tracing, raw-socket network intelligence, GPU-accelerated LLM inference, and sovereign containerized ecosystems. I architect for production from day one — resilient, observable, and scalable.

I consult on technical strategy, system architecture, remote sensing workflows, and AI integration for clients in aerospace, environmental monitoring, and enterprise intelligence sectors.
